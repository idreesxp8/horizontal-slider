More-meaningful customization

Legacy is backed by a strong partner, Bain Capital Credit, and will also have two leverage facilities, one with a major commercial bank and the other with a strong non-bank financial. This means we can offer a wide range of customized loans nationwide in which the structure and cost of funding are optimized to the situation of the borrower.

More funding

Our long experience with ABL, and all types of collateral, means we may offer higher advance rates on a wider range of collateral types —providing more liquidity for borrowers without the limits or restrictions of bank loans.

More certainty over time

Our platform’s capital strength does more than provide assurance of closing—it is a resource that may be called on during the life of a loan when borrowers may have additional needs or opportunities.

More speed

We are a streamlined operation, and our decision-makers are close to every transaction from day one. We are quick to create solutions to meet client needs while banks are still trying to understand the opportunity.

More accessibility

We believe in being reachable. Our originators, underwriters, and servicers are experienced and knowledgeable. Want to talk to one of the partners? Just call!

More support in times of stress

Our business model is based on our belief that over time, an asset-based lender will be a more successful company when it sees its customers not just as counterparties, but as partners, with a growth mindset on their behalf and an optimistically supportive view of their special borrowing needs.

The people at Legacy Corporate Lending have been making asset-based loans for a long time—and when there’s an issue with a loan, we don’t panic. We know that businesses experience hiccups. We roll up our sleeves right along with them and work to find a path toward success.
